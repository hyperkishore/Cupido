# Retreat Conversation Canvas

This canvas is structured around **four themed decks** adapted from So Much Closer’s style: **DIVE, SEEN, Emotional Intelligence, and HOA**. Each section provides 20 curated questions designed to help successful entrepreneurs in their 30s–40s build meaningful connections during the retreat.

---

## **DIVE – Self-Discovery**
1. What’s a belief you’ve outgrown that once defined you?  
2. Which life decision are you proudest of — and why?  
3. When did you last feel completely outside your comfort zone?  
4. What unfinished dream still lingers in you?  
5. If your teenage self saw you now, what would surprise them most?  
6. What personal value have you never compromised on?  
7. When was the last time you changed your mind about something important?  
8. How do you know when you’re truly at peace?  
9. What part of your identity feels misunderstood by others?  
10. Which habits serve you best — and which quietly sabotage you?  
11. What role does fear play in your decision-making?  
12. If you had a “life thesis statement,” what would it say?  
13. What failure taught you the deepest lesson?  
14. Who is a mirror in your life — someone who reflects truths you can’t see?  
15. When do you feel most like yourself?  
16. What is a boundary you’ve recently set that empowered you?  
17. Which compliment do you secretly wish to hear?  
18. What risk would you take if you knew you couldn’t fail?  
19. What does fulfillment mean to you at this stage of life?  
20. How would you describe the chapter of life you’re in right now?  

---

## **SEEN – Healing & Deeper Relationships**
1. What do you wish people asked you about more often?  
2. When was the last time you felt truly seen?  
3. What’s one apology you wish you’d received?  
4. What’s one apology you wish you’d given?  
5. How do you show love when words fall short?  
6. What childhood wound still echoes in your adult life?  
7. When do you feel most vulnerable in relationships?  
8. What’s a truth about yourself you find hard to share?  
9. How do you usually respond to conflict — and what would you like to do differently?  
10. Which relationship in your life has shaped you the most?  
11. What’s one way someone made you feel safe recently?  
12. What emotion do you hide most often from others?  
13. When did forgiveness change your life?  
14. What’s your love language, and how did you discover it?  
15. How do you rebuild trust once it’s broken?  
16. Who in your life challenges you to grow?  
17. When do you feel loneliest, even when surrounded by people?  
18. What’s a story about you that others often misinterpret?  
19. What’s one thing you wish your family understood about you?  
20. What do you hope people remember about how you made them feel?  

---

## **Emotional Intelligence**
1. What emotion is hardest for you to regulate, and why?  
2. When do you feel most in control of your emotions?  
3. What typically triggers you, and how do you work through it?  
4. How do you pause before reacting when you’re upset?  
5. What motivates you when external rewards disappear?  
6. How do you recover after an emotionally draining day?  
7. What’s your process for calming yourself when anxious?  
8. When did empathy change how you approached a situation?  
9. How do you know when you’ve truly listened to someone?  
10. How do you identify the emotions beneath anger?  
11. What’s one situation where your emotions taught you more than logic?  
12. When do you find it easiest to connect to someone else’s feelings?  
13. What feedback about your emotional presence has stuck with you?  
14. How do you recharge your emotional energy?  
15. When do you feel inspired, and how do you sustain it?  
16. What’s your strategy for staying motivated during setbacks?  
17. How do you cultivate empathy for someone you disagree with?  
18. When has emotional honesty helped you at work?  
19. How do you express gratitude in a way that feels natural?  
20. What’s the biggest emotional skill you’re working on developing right now?  

---

## **HOA – Values & Life Philosophy**
1. What core value guides most of your choices?  
2. Which value do you admire in others but struggle to embody yourself?  
3. When did your personal values clash with societal expectations?  
4. What philosophy of life are you living by — consciously or unconsciously?  
5. What role does money play in your life philosophy?  
6. Which value do you hope your children (or next generation) inherit from you?  
7. When did you most clearly live out your values under pressure?  
8. What is a “non-negotiable” in your relationships?  
9. Which mentor or figure has shaped your values the most?  
10. What does integrity mean to you in action?  
11. When have you compromised a value, and what did it cost you?  
12. What philosophy helps you make sense of suffering?  
13. How do your values influence your leadership style?  
14. Which personal principle do you revisit often to course-correct?  
15. What legacy value do you want to be remembered for?  
16. How do you balance ambition with contentment?  
17. Which book, teaching, or philosophy reshaped your worldview?  
18. What value do you wish society honored more deeply?  
19. How do you define “a good life” for yourself?  
20. If you wrote a manifesto of your life, what would the first line say?  

---

### How to Use This Canvas
- Break into small groups, each with a different deck theme.
- Pick questions at random to spark conversations.
- Encourage depth, vulnerability, and curiosity — not speed.
- Rotate groups so participants experience all four themes.

This structure ensures **meaningful, engaging conversations** that help leaders connect on values, emotions, and personal journeys beyond business.
