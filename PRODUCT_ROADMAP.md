# Cupido: Product Roadmap & Requirements Document (PRD)
*The World's Most Engaging Self-Discovery & Partner-Finding Platform*

## Executive Summary

Cupido is revolutionizing how people discover themselves and form authentic connections. Unlike traditional dating apps that focus on superficial attraction, Cupido uses AI-powered conversational intelligence to help users understand their deepest selves while connecting them with compatible partners based on personality, values, and growth potential.

**Mission:** Create the world's most thoughtful platform for self-discovery and meaningful relationship formation through authentic conversation and AI-powered insights.

**Vision:** Every person deserves to understand themselves deeply and find someone who appreciates their authentic self while growing together.
